About This Site

This site was created by Elvijs Stepens for a course at Riga Technical University (RTU). It is designed to help users calculate the maximum mortgage loan amount they can afford based on their net income and other financial factors. The calculator follows the guidelines set by the Bank of Latvia and the Consumer Rights Protection Centre of Latvia.

The purpose of this project is to provide an informative and easy-to-use tool for potential borrowers to understand their borrowing capacity and make informed financial decisions.

Features of this site include:

  • Calculation of maximum mortgage loan amount based on net income and other factors
  • Consideration of dependants and existing loan obligations
  • Information on borrower-based macroprudential measures
