About This Site

This site was created for a course at Fundamentals of Computer Science and Programming. It is designed to help users calculate the maximum mortgage loan amount they can afford based on their net income and other financial factors. The calculator follows the guidelines set by the Bank of Latvia and the Consumer Rights Protection Centre of Latvia.

The purpose of this project is to provide an informative and easy-to-use tool for potential borrowers to understand their borrowing capacity and make informed financial decisions.

If you have any questions or feedback, please contact Elvijs at elvijs.stepens@edu.rtu.lv.
